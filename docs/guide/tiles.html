Tiles

Tiles are not any different from cards. to create a tile from card, you will need to add the classes, .ad-block to remove the border-radius || .ad-flat to remove the shadow effect || .no-margin to remove the margin that comes with the card. and finally, there are not formats for paragraphs and titles in a tiles. The tile is best used in making a gallery of images, with no margins and paddding, and also for container. Like cards, they are declared in a column with class .ad-tile
